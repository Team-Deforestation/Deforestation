What is Deforestation?

Deforesation is the clearing of Forest or wood lands done by humans such as mining, agriculture, logging, oil & gas extraction, cattle ranching and agriculture. These Lands are usually converted into agriculture land, urban areas or barren land.

What are the consequences?

Loss of biodiversity and extinction in animals, plants and microbes. Soil Erosion, nutrients in top soil washed away. Desertification, these areas turn into a hot and dry climate. Climate change and pollution, carbon dioxide released into the atmosphere increasing the rate of global warming.
